Course Description

As a Graphic designer your skill is always in demand by businesses and orgnisations seeking to create and maintain their visual imageries.
Through this course you will learn fundamental design concepts that wil enable you to design attractive design content such as flyers (for online and print),and brand identities.

You will learn and master CorelDraw and Adobe Photoshop to enable you create beautiful and professional Designs.
